# Master Content Plan

## Catholic Veritas

This document is the master blueprint for the entire Catholic Veritas knowledge base. It defines the full conceptual structure of the finished project and serves as the long-term table of contents for a Catholic apologetics and theological reference library that may ultimately contain between 2,000 and 10,000 high-quality articles.

This document is not a content inventory and it is not a roadmap. It does not track what already exists or describe the order in which pages should be written. Instead, it describes the full shape of the mature knowledge base so that Catholic Veritas can grow for decades without losing structural clarity or requiring major reorganisation.

Its purpose is to define every major knowledge domain, doctrinal area, apologetics subject, historical field, biblical topic, and reference collection that Catholic Veritas should eventually contain.

Every article must still function independently, but the knowledge base becomes stronger when foundational content exists first.

Examples of desirable dependencies:

## Future Expansion

Entirely new subject areas should be added by strengthening existing knowledge domains whenever possible.

Future growth should follow these principles:

- expand downward before expanding outward
- add subdomains before adding top-level domains
- create deeper branches within stable categories
- preserve one canonical home for each major subject
- use cross-links and reference collections to absorb complexity

New top-level categories should be introduced only when a major body of content truly cannot fit naturally within existing domains. Most future growth should occur by deepening current structures rather than multiplying new ones.

This approach allows Catholic Veritas to grow substantially without losing clarity, usability, or architectural stability.

## Vision of the Finished Knowledge Base

After ten years of disciplined development, Catholic Veritas should stand as one of the world’s most trusted, organized, and comprehensive Catholic apologetics and theological knowledge bases.

The finished knowledge base should contain:

- a complete and stable doctrinal framework
- broad and deep biblical coverage
- extensive apologetics treatment
- rich Church history and patristic resources
- strong reference tools
- carefully structured cross-category relationships
- clear entry points for beginners and deep pathways for advanced readers

It should function as a long-term Catholic reference library that readers can use for study, research, evangelization, apologetics, teaching, and clarification of difficult questions.

Its greatest strength should not be mere size, but coherent design, faithful structure, durable usefulness, and disciplined growth. Catholic Veritas should become not just large, but deeply reliable, navigable, and intellectually serious.
